Annotation 1: Between 2017 and 2018, the address at 116th and Broadway 2955 went from being occupied by Vine Sushi & Sake, a non-chain restaurant, to Pret a Manger, a national chain.

Annotation 2: Of the 39 non-chain restaurants on Broadway in 2008, 11 left their Broadway address at some point in the last 13 years. Between 2008 and 2021, the percentage of restaurants on Broadway that were national chains or New York City chains increased from 32 percent% to 47 percent%.

Annotation 3: Between 2008 and 2021, the percentage of national and New York City chain restaurants on Amsterdam increased from 9 percent% to 22%. In contrast, the percentage of national and NYC chain restaurants on Columbus Avenue and other surrounding areas decreased from 15.5% to 12.5% between 2008 and 2021, with the majority of new restaurants in the area being non-chain restaurants.

Methodology- Using Google Street View, we “walked” down the streets in the Columbia area and noted what restaurant occupied each space from 2008 through 2019. For most data points, Street View versions were available for 2008, 2009, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017, and 2019. We then determined whether these restaurants were single storefronts, NYC-based chains, or national chains.
